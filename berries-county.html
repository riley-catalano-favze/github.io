Who We Are

The Agency Built for Berrien County’s Tri-State Economy.

Berrien County occupies a geography that no other Michigan county can claim: the southwestern corner of the state, sharing borders with Indiana and Illinois, anchored by 76 miles of Lake Michigan shoreline, and positioned directly between the Chicago metropolitan area and South Bend’s Michiana region. Niles is the county seat, a manufacturing and healthcare hub whose commercial gravity extends into St. Joseph County, Indiana. Benton Harbor carries the legacy of Whirlpool Corporation, one of America’s great manufacturing companies, and an arts district that has attracted national attention as a model for mid-sized city reinvestment. The Lake Michigan shoreline communities from Union Pier through New Buffalo and Bridgman pull significant Chicago-area visitor traffic and second-home investment. Andrews University in Berrien Springs brings an international population to an otherwise rural agricultural community. Eau Claire anchors the county’s fruit-growing interior, part of Michigan’s fruit belt running along the Lake Michigan climate corridor. Berrien County is not a saturated digital marketing market. Most categories across the county, including in Niles and Benton Harbor, are effectively unclaimed in Local SEO, AEO, and AI-driven search. The first businesses to build genuine presence will hold those positions for years.
