What Favze Does For You

Everything Included — From Strategy to Results

Favze handles every piece of your paid social campaigns. Strategy, creative, targeting, management, optimization, and reporting. You run your business — we run your ads.

01
Strategy
Strategic Campaign Planning & Development
We start by thoroughly understanding your business, your ideal customer, and your goals — not just picking platforms and running ads blindly. Every campaign is built on a foundation of real market knowledge.
  • Deep discovery of your business, goals, and ideal customer
  • Platform selection based on where your audience actually is
  • Audience research and targeting strategy
  • Campaign structure design (objectives, ad sets, budget)
  • Custom, lookalike, and competitor audience building
  • Exclusion audiences to prevent wasted spend
02
Creative
High-Performance Ad Creative
We write copy that stops the scroll and create visuals that perform. Getting a click is only possible when your ad earns attention first — and we build creative that does exactly that, tested across multiple variations to find what works.
  • Attention-grabbing headlines that stop the scroll
  • Benefit-focused copy tailored to Northern Indiana audiences
  • Static image, carousel, and video ad creation
  • Platform-specific format optimization
  • Multiple variations for A/B testing
  • Continuous creative refresh to prevent ad fatigue
03
Targeting
Advanced Targeting & Retargeting
Precision targeting is what separates social ads from traditional advertising. We build layered audiences — geographic, demographic, interest, behavioral, and custom — so every dollar targets people most likely to become customers.
  • Geographic targeting specific to your service area
  • Lookalike audiences based on your best customers
  • Website visitor retargeting
  • Social media engager re-engagement
  • Customer list matching and exclusion
  • Strategic exclusions to eliminate wasted spend
04
Management
Comprehensive Campaign Management
Active, daily campaign management — not set-it-and-forget-it. We monitor performance, adjust budgets, pause underperformers, and scale winners continuously. Your campaigns get better over time because we never stop optimizing.
  • Daily spend and performance monitoring
  • Weekly budget adjustments based on results
  • Bidding strategy optimization
  • Scaling what works, cutting what doesn't
  • Seasonal strategy adjustments
  • Monthly strategy calls and performance reviews
05
Tracking
Conversion Tracking & Detailed Reporting
You'll always know exactly what your campaigns delivered. We set up complete conversion tracking from day one — and give you monthly reports in plain language so you can see the ROI without needing to be a marketing expert to understand it.
  • Platform pixel installation and verification
  • Phone call, form, and purchase conversion tracking
  • Cost per lead and cost per customer tracking
  • Return on ad spend (ROAS) measurement
  • Plain-language monthly performance reports
  • Audience and creative performance insights
