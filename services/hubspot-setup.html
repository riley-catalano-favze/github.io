The 4 Hubs

Everything Your Business Needs — One Platform

Most businesses buy HubSpot and use 15% of it. Favze configures all four hubs — CRM, Marketing, Sales, and Service — so every part of your business works together, with your Northern Indiana customer at the center.

01
Hub 01
CRM & Contact Management
● Foundation

Your CRM is the backbone of HubSpot. We configure it so every contact, company, deal, and interaction is tracked automatically — giving your Goshen or Elkhart team a single, accurate view of every customer relationship without manual data entry.

  • Contact and company properties customized to your specific business data needs
  • Lifecycle stages configured to match your actual buyer's journey
  • Lead scoring model built around what actually predicts conversion for your business
  • User accounts, teams, and permission levels for your entire team
  • Data migration from spreadsheets, legacy CRMs, or other platforms — clean from day one
  • Duplicate removal, field mapping, and relationship associations after import
  • Gmail or Outlook integration — emails logged automatically, no manual entry
  • Website tracking code installed — every visitor action tied back to your contacts

Why this matters first: Every other hub depends on clean CRM data. Getting this right from the start prevents the data quality problems that make most HubSpot implementations fail after six months.

02
Hub 02
Marketing Hub
● Automation

Marketing Hub turns your outreach from manual and inconsistent into automated and intelligent. We build the workflows, email sequences, and nurture programs that keep your Northern Indiana prospects engaged — from first touch through first sale — without you lifting a finger each time.

  • Email sending domain authentication — DKIM, SPF, and DMARC for strong deliverability
  • Lead nurturing workflows — welcome series, follow-ups, re-engagement sequences
  • Forms with progressive profiling — capture the right info without friction
  • Landing page templates optimized for conversion and mobile responsiveness
  • Ad tracking integration — Google Ads and social campaigns tied to actual revenue
  • Subscription type management — CAN-SPAM and GDPR compliant from setup
  • eCommerce integration — Shopify and WooCommerce connected for purchase tracking, abandoned cart recovery, and repeat purchase automation
  • Contact list segmentation by geography, behavior, lifecycle stage, and custom properties
  • Campaign analytics and attribution — see which marketing efforts generate real revenue

The automation difference: A well-built Marketing Hub workflow nurtures leads at 2am on a Sunday the same way your best salesperson would at 9am Monday — consistently, personally, and without burning anyone out.

03
Hub 03
Sales Hub
● Close More

Sales Hub gives your team everything they need to move deals forward faster — without the admin work that slows them down. We build the pipeline stages, automation, and tools that match how your Northern Indiana business actually sells, so your reps spend time selling instead of updating spreadsheets.

  • Deal pipeline built to match your actual sales stages — not a generic default
  • Email templates with personalization tokens — consistent outreach at scale
  • Sales sequences — automated multi-touch follow-up so no lead falls through the cracks
  • Meeting scheduling links with calendar sync — no back-and-forth scheduling emails
  • Task automation — follow-up reminders created automatically based on deal stage
  • Quote templates and proposal workflows — from opportunity to signed deal faster
  • Sales analytics — pipeline velocity, win/loss tracking, rep performance
  • Revenue forecasting so your business can plan and hire with confidence

The compounding advantage: Every month of Sales Hub usage builds historical data that improves forecasting accuracy and reveals the exact behaviors that predict closed deals in your specific market.

04
Hub 04
Service Hub
● Retain More

Service Hub turns your customer support from a cost center into a growth driver. We configure the ticketing system, feedback loops, and knowledge base that help Northern Indiana businesses resolve issues faster, collect more reviews, and turn satisfied customers into repeat buyers and referral sources.

  • Ticketing pipeline — every issue tracked from open to resolved, no dropped balls
  • Automated ticket routing and SLA management — right person gets the right ticket
  • Knowledge base setup — customers find answers themselves, support volume drops
  • NPS, CSAT, and CES surveys triggered automatically at key moments
  • Live chat and chatbot configured for your website — instant responses, qualified leads
  • Conversation inbox — email, chat, and calls in one place, never miss a customer message
  • Post-service review request automation — more Google reviews without more manual effort
  • Service analytics — resolution times, customer satisfaction trends, rep performance

The retention multiplier: Acquiring a new customer costs 5–7x more than retaining one. Service Hub gives you the systems to keep every customer happy, visible, and coming back — which is the highest ROI lever in your business.
