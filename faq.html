09 - AI Search Visibility: SEO, AEO & GEO

The Three Layers of Modern Search

SEO alone is no longer enough. Favze covers all three ecosystems. Read the full breakdown in Favze Insights.

Because the way customers find businesses has fractured into three completely distinct ecosystems - traditional search, AI-powered answer surfaces, and generative AI platforms. A brand can hold strong Google rankings and still experience declining traffic because users are getting answers directly from Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini without ever clicking a link. SEO is the foundation, but AEO and GEO have become non-negotiable layers on top of it.
SEO - Search Engine Optimization - is optimizing your website so traditional search engines like Google and Bing rank your pages highly and send you clicks. It has three pillars that must all work together. Technical SEO is the infrastructure: site speed, mobile responsiveness, crawl health, Core Web Vitals, structured data, and architecture. On-page SEO is the content and relevance layer: keyword strategy, heading structure, content depth, and topical authority. Off-page SEO is the authority layer: backlinks from credible sources, digital PR, and brand signals across the web. Without a clean technical foundation, the rest of your SEO investment underperforms.
AEO is optimizing your content to be extracted and presented as the direct answer in AI-powered search features - Google AI Overviews, featured snippets, People Also Ask boxes, and voice search through Siri, Alexa, and Google Assistant. In SEO, you optimize to be found. In AEO, you optimize to be the answer. That requires question-based headings that mirror how people actually ask, concise direct answers immediately after each question, and proper schema markup - especially FAQ schema - so Google can machine-read your content as structured Q&A pairs. Vague or hedging answers do not get extracted. Direct, declarative answers do.
GEO is building your brand's content authority so that generative AI platforms - ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, Meta AI - incorporate your brand and your expertise into the synthesized responses they generate for users. These platforms do not show a list of links. They generate an answer. If your brand isn't in their training data or retrieval systems as a credible, frequently-cited authority, you don't exist in those responses regardless of your Google rankings. GEO is the most misunderstood of the three disciplines and the one where most businesses currently have the widest gap.
AEO targets AI-enhanced features inside traditional search engines - primarily Google AI Overviews and Bing Copilot search results. GEO targets standalone generative AI platforms like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini, where users conduct entire research sessions through conversational AI rather than through a search engine at all. AEO is about extraction from search results. GEO is about inclusion in synthesized intelligence. They require overlapping but distinct strategies.
E-E-A-T stands for Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness. Google formalized this framework for evaluating content quality, and generative AI systems use the same underlying logic to determine which sources deserve to be surfaced. Experience signals firsthand knowledge. Expertise signals deep domain command. Authoritativeness signals external validation from credible sources. Trustworthiness signals accuracy and integrity. In GEO, E-E-A-T is not one factor among many - it is the primary determinant of whether a brand gets cited or ignored. Building genuine E-E-A-T is a long-term investment, not a content trick.
Favze approaches AI visibility across all three layers simultaneously. For AEO, we restructure content with question-format headings and direct answers, implement FAQ schema and HowTo schema, and build an answer-optimized content architecture. For GEO, we build E-E-A-T authority through original research, digital PR placements in authoritative publications, consistent brand entity management across the web, and deep content that generative AI systems weight as a primary source. We also monitor your brand's presence directly inside ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude to measure where you appear, in what context, and how that changes over time. Most agencies don't offer any of this yet.
Yes - but only with deliberate multi-layer architecture. Content built for all three combines long-form depth and keyword optimization for SEO, embedded FAQ sections with schema markup and concise direct answers for AEO, and original research with expert voice for GEO citation potential. Content that achieves all three simultaneously is the highest-leverage investment in search visibility a business can make. It is exactly the kind of content architecture Favze specializes in building.
