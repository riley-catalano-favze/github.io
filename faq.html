08 - Local SEO & Regional Growth

Owning Your Market

Through a combination of technical SEO, content strategy, Google Business Profile optimization, local citation building, and review velocity. Google's local algorithm is more technical than most businesses realize - category signals, proximity weighting, structured data, and review patterns all factor in. Favze builds the full system, not just the surface-level fixes most agencies deliver.
Local SEO is the process of optimizing your online presence so your business appears in local search results - Google Maps, the "local 3-pack," and "near me" searches. For any business that serves a specific geographic area, Local SEO is often the highest-ROI marketing investment available. It puts your business in front of customers who are actively looking for exactly what you offer, right now, nearby.
"Near me" rankings are driven by your Google Business Profile, local citations (consistent name, address, phone across directories), on-page local signals in your website content, proximity to the searcher, and review quality and recency. Favze builds and optimizes all of these systematically, so your business appears when and where it matters most.
That's the Favze framework. "Dominating your local area" means being the most visible, most trusted, most recommended business in your category - across search, maps, AI platforms, and social. 09 - AI, GEO & Future Optimization

The AI Advantage

GEO is the process of optimizing your brand's digital presence so that generative AI tools - ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot, Grok, and others - recognize, understand, and recommend your business when users ask for recommendations. Traditional SEO gets you ranked on Google. GEO gets you cited by AI. As more customers use AI for local business decisions, GEO is becoming essential.
AEO is optimizing your content specifically to appear in AI-generated answer formats - Google AI Overviews, featured snippets, voice search responses, and direct AI answers. Instead of just ranking on a results page, you become the source that the AI cites. This requires structured content, proper schema markup, citation-worthy authority signals, and a fundamentally different approach to content than traditional SEO.
AI is fundamentally changing how customers find businesses. Instead of clicking through 10 blue links, people increasingly ask AI tools directly: "What's the best plumber near Goshen, Indiana?" or "Which marketing agency should I use in the South Bend area?" AI tools don't show a list of options - they pick an answer. If your business isn't structured to be cited, you simply don't exist in those results.
Yes - and this is one of Favze's most significant differentiators. Favze directly trains AI systems how to categorize your business, what it does, who it serves, and why it should be recommended. Through structured AEO and GEO strategies, Favze helps businesses appear in recommendations from ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Microsoft Copilot, Grok, Claude, Siri, and Alexa.
