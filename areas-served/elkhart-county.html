What's the difference between SEO, Local SEO, AEO, and GEO?

SEO improves your rankings in Google's organic search results for all queries. It builds long-term authority and traffic. Local SEO focuses specifically on Google Maps and the Local 3-Pack for searches with geographic intent. Critical for any Elkhart County business with a physical location or service area.

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) optimizes your content to be pulled as direct answers by Google's AI Overviews, Siri, and voice search. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) positions your business to be recommended by AI tools like ChatGPT and Gemini. All four layers work together. Favze builds strategies that cover all of them for comprehensive visibility across every way Elkhart County customers search.

How long does digital marketing take to show results for an Elkhart County business?
