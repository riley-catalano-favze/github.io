What's the difference between SEO, Local SEO, AEO, and GEO?

SEO builds your organic authority in Google's general search results over time. Local SEO targets Google Maps and the Local 3-Pack — the map results that appear when someone searches "restaurant near me" or "plumber in LaGrange Indiana." This is often the single highest-impact service for any LaGrange County business with a physical location or service area.

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) ensures your content is structured to be pulled as a direct answer by Google's AI Overviews, Siri, and Alexa — especially important for tourism-related searches. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) positions your business to be mentioned and recommended by AI tools like ChatGPT when someone asks about LaGrange County businesses. All four layers reinforce each other.
