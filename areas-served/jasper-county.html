Who We Are

The Agency Built for Jasper County's Distinct Markets.

Jasper County occupies a strategically valuable position in northwest Indiana: it runs directly along I-65, one of the busiest north-south corridors in the Midwest, connecting Indianapolis to Chicago. That geography creates market dynamics that most rural Indiana counties simply don't have. DeMotte, in the county's northwest corner, functions as a bedroom community for Chicago-area workers and captures significant interstate commerce. Remington, on the eastern edge, sits squarely on I-65's daily traffic flow. Rensselaer anchors the county's civic and commercial core with St. Joseph's College driving year-round academic and community demand. Most Jasper County businesses are completely invisible to the customers — both local residents and I-65 travelers — who are actively searching for their services online right now.

The I-65 Corridor Opportunity
Tens of thousands of vehicles travel I-65 through Jasper County every day between Indianapolis and Chicago. Travelers making fuel stops, meal decisions, and overnight lodging choices do so based on what appears at the top of Google Maps and search results — not by driving around looking for signs. A DeMotte restaurant, Remington gas station, or any business near an I-65 interchange that fails to appear in those search results loses customers that are already physically nearby. For Jasper County businesses with any I-65 exposure, Local SEO and Google Business Profile optimization are among the highest-return investments available anywhere in Indiana.

Rensselaer — the county seat — runs on its own distinct engine. St. Joseph's College anchors the city's academic calendar with students, faculty, and staff driving demand for food, services, retail, and off-campus housing. The broader county commercial base serves the agricultural townships that make Jasper County one of Indiana's most productive farming regions. These rural communities are economically active and digitally underserved in virtually every service category.

Favze is based in Middlebury in Elkhart County and operates across the Northern and Northwest Indiana market corridor that includes Jasper County. We understand how this region functions — from the college-driven commercial rhythms of Rensselaer to the interstate traveler economy that passes through DeMotte and Remington every day. That regional fluency is built into every strategy we build for Jasper County clients.
