Who We Are

The Agency Built for Porter County’s Three Distinct Markets.

Porter County is one of the most economically varied counties in Northwest Indiana. Valparaiso is a college town that has matured into a genuine regional commercial and professional services hub, anchored by Valparaiso University and a downtown that draws buyers from across the county and beyond. Portage is Porter County’s most populous city, built around working-class residential density, industrial employment, and a commercial corridor serving a high-volume local consumer base. Chesterton occupies a unique position as the entry point to Indiana Dunes National Park, a community of roughly 14,000 residents that experiences dramatic seasonal tourist traffic from Chicago and the broader Midwest. Each of these communities requires a completely different digital marketing strategy, and a single generic approach applied county-wide will fail in all three.

Porter County’s Economy. Three Commercially Distinct Markets Under One Roof.
Valparaiso carries the county’s highest concentration of professional services, healthcare, dining, and specialty retail, with a well-educated consumer base that researches thoroughly before spending. Portage delivers density, with large residential neighborhoods and a working-family consumer base that makes frequent, practical purchasing decisions across home services, automotive, food and beverage, and retail. Chesterton captures both a loyal year-round local base and significant seasonal visitor traffic from Indiana Dunes National Park, creating a tourism-adjacent economy that most small businesses in the community have not learned to capitalize on digitally.
