The Agency Built for Northern Indiana’s Real Economy.

Northern Indiana does not function like a single market. Elkhart County is the RV manufacturing capital of the world, with a supplier network that extends across the region and a workforce that commutes from six counties in every direction. St. Joseph County anchors the Michiana metro, drawing from both sides of the Indiana-Michigan state line. LaGrange County’s Amish Country generates millions in agritourism annually, yet most of its small businesses have no digital presence to capture visitors who plan their trips entirely online. Kosciusko County’s lake communities attract seasonal visitors and second-home owners who search for local services before they arrive. Allen County is Fort Wayne, Indiana’s second-largest city, where professional services, healthcare, and manufacturing all compete in a market still dramatically underdeveloped digitally outside the city’s most competitive categories. Across all 18 counties we serve, the pattern is the same: real commercial activity, genuine customer demand, and very little digital marketing capturing it. The businesses that move first win category positions they will hold for years.
