Google Search Ads
● Live Now

When someone in Goshen, Elkhart, or South Bend searches for what you offer on Google, your ad appears at the top — above organic results — reaching people at the exact moment they're ready to act. You pay per click, and every dollar is tracked against real business outcomes.

  • Responsive Search Ads with 15 headlines & 4 descriptions tested by Google AI

The key advantage: A higher Quality Score means Favze can get your ads ranked above higher-bidding competitors while paying less per click. We build campaigns engineered for Quality Score from day one.

02
Platform 02
Google Local Service Ads
● Live Now

LSAs flip the PPC model entirely. Instead of paying every time someone clicks — whether they buy or not — you only pay when a verified customer in your service area calls or messages you directly. They appear above everything else on Google, including regular ads, and carry the Google Verified badge for instant trust.

  • Pay per verified lead only — real customers, not random clicks
  • Appears above all Google results — above the Map Pack, above regular ads
  • Google Verified badge — license, insurance & background checks build immediate trust
  • Full verification & badge setup — we guide you through every step
  • Rankings driven by reviews & responsiveness — great work beats big budgets
  • Dispute management — spam calls, wrong area, duplicate leads all disputed for credits
  • Service area targeting across Elkhart County, St. Joseph, Kosciusko & beyond
  • Monthly lead review, budget adjustment, and performance reporting

LSA window closing: Rankings are shaped by historical performance — the longer you've been active, the harder you are to displace. Businesses that start LSAs now build an advantage competitors who wait can't easily erase.

Know The Difference

Google Search Ads vs. Local Service Ads

Both are Google products. Both put you at the top. But they work very differently — and for local service businesses, LSAs often deliver a lower cost per customer. Understanding both is how you build a smarter paid search strategy.

Standard PPC
Google Search Ads
Best for any business, any industry. Full control over targeting, bidding, and creative.
How You Pay
Per click — browser, bot, or buyer. No conversion still costs you.
Page Position
Below LSA results. On mobile, users often scroll past before reaching your ad.
Trust Signal
No verification badge. Anyone can run an ad — customers can't tell who's licensed.
Ranking Factor
Bid + Quality Score. Bigger budgets generally win better positions.
Bad Leads
No dispute process. Wrong clicks eat your budget with no recourse.
Best For
Any business. E-commerce, lead gen, brand awareness, all industries.
Pay Per Lead
Google Local Service Ads
Purpose-built for local service businesses. Only available to eligible categories.
How You Pay
Per verified lead only — a real person in your area called or messaged you.
Page Position
Above everything — Map Pack, regular ads, all organic results. #1 placement.
Trust Signal
Google Verified badge — license, insurance & background check shown before they call.
Ranking Factor
Reviews + responsiveness. Great work and fast responses beat bigger budgets.
Bad Leads
Dispute process available. Spam, wrong area, duplicate leads credited back.
Best For
Local service businesses — HVAC, plumbing, legal, cleaning, marketing agencies, and more.
